The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 envisions a future where technology, sustainability, and innovation drive progress. With a focus on digital transformation, green energy, and skilled workforce development, it aims to foster inclusive growth while addressing climate challenges, emerging as a leader in regional and global cooperation.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Three Zeros" vision aspires to create a world free from poverty, unemployment, and environmental harm. By promoting social business, self-employment, and green technologies, this vision seeks to build a sustainable and inclusive society where economic and environmental well-being go hand in hand.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus
Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh

Recent News

Launched in May 2018, Bangabandhu-1 is Bangladesh's first geostationary communications satellite. It was designed to provide a range of services, including direct-to-home (DTH) broadcasting, VSAT communications, backhaul connectivity, and disaster preparedness.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Despite the challenges faced with Bangabandhu-1, Bangladesh is moving forward with plans to launch a second satellite. Officials have stated that the deal for this new satellite is expected to be finalized within the next two to three months, with a projected launch timeline of two to three years. This initiative aims to enhance the country's capabilities in communication and data services.
